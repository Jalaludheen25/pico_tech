# Website Documentation: Candela Medical (candelamedical.com)

## 1. Executive Summary
[Candela Medical](https://candelamedical.com/) is a leading global medical aesthetic device company that provides clinically-backed technology and support systems for aesthetic practices. The website serves as a comprehensive hub for medical providers to explore technologies, access training, and manage their practice's marketing and technical needs.

## 2. Company Overview
- **Founded:** 1970
- **Headquarters:** Marlborough, Massachusetts, USA
- **CEO:** Geoffrey Crouse
- **Core Values:** Science, Results, and Trust.
- **Mission:** To inform, inspire, and empower innovations that lift the medical aesthetic industry and the practices it serves.
- **Global Presence:** Operates in multiple countries with various regional offices and a global distribution network.

## 3. Product Portfolio
Candela's product line is categorized by technology and clinical application:

| Product Series | Key Technology | Clinical Applications |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gentle Pro™** | Alexandrite/Nd:YAG Laser | Laser hair removal (Gold Standard), vascularity, and wrinkles. |
| **PicoWay®** | Picosecond Laser | Tattoo removal, acne scars, and wrinkle treatment. |
| **Nordlys™** | Ellipse IPL / Nd:YAG / Frax | Vascularity, pigmentation, and skin resurfacing. |
| **Matrix™** | RF Microneedling | Skin tightening and collagen stimulation. |
| **Vbeam®** | Pulsed Dye Laser (PDL) | Vascular and dermatological conditions. |
| **Glacē™** | Multi-step Facial | Skin cleansing, exfoliation, and hydration. |

## 4. Resource & Support Center
Candela offers a robust ecosystem of support for its partners (providers):
- **Training (Candela Institute of Excellence):** Clinical training, workshops, and certificate programs.
- **Marketing Tools:** Downloadable assets to help clinics promote Candela treatments.
- **Clinical Library:** Access to white papers, clinical studies, and evidence-based research.
- **Webinars:** On-demand educational content for clinical and business growth.
- **Customer Portal:** A unified dashboard for order management and technical support.

## 5. Target Audience & Engagement
- **Medical Providers:** Surgeons, dermatologists, and med-spa owners seeking advanced aesthetic technology.
- **Patients/Consumers:** Individuals looking for effective aesthetic treatments.
- **Provider Engagement:** Through the **Provider Shop** and dedicated portal access.
- **Patient Engagement:** Via the [Customer/Patient Site](https://candelamedical.com/patient) and "Find a Provider" directory.

## 6. Website Navigation Structure
### Main Navigation
- **Products:** Browse by category, technology, or specific clinical treatment.
- **Resources:** Access to the Clinical Library, Marketing Center, and Webinars.
- **Upcoming Events:** Calendar of tradeshows and regional workshops.
- **Company:** About Us, Career opportunities, and Press Center.

### Utility & Footer
- **Portals:** Quick links to the Provider Shop and Customer Portal.
- **Contact:** Global contact forms for sales and support.
- **Legal:** Privacy policies, Cookie management, and Terms of Use.
