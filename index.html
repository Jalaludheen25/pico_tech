Picosecond Technology

Ultra-Short Pulses.
Maximum Impact.

The Pico Laser delivers energy in ultra-short 450-picosecond pulses — creating a predominantly photoacoustic effect that shatters pigment and tattoo ink without heating surrounding tissue. This means better results with fewer treatments and minimal downtime for patients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend PicoTech training programs?
Training programs are open to licensed medical professionals including dermatologists, plastic surgeons, aesthetic physicians, and registered nurses working under physician supervision. All attendees must provide proof of medical licensing.
Are the programs available in Arabic?
Yes! PicoTech offers training programs in both English and Arabic. Our UAE-based clinical trainers are bilingual, and all printed course materials are available in both languages.
Where are training sessions held?
Primary training sessions are held at our Dubai Healthcare City training center. We also offer on-site training at your clinic and regional workshops across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, and the wider GCC region.
Do I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes. All program graduates receive a PicoTech Institute of Excellence certificate of completion, which is recognized by healthcare authorities in the UAE and GCC. Certificates are a prerequisite for applying to our Provider Directory.
Is training included when I purchase a device?
Foundation-level clinical training and device setup training are included with every device purchase. Advanced programs and specialized workshops may be offered at a subsidized rate for PicoTech device owners.
